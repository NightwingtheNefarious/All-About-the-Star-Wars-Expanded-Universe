  • Biography of Luke Skywalker

Luke Skywalker was a young farmer, who lived on the planet Tatooine with his Uncle Owen and Aunt Beru. When Luke's aunt and uncle were killled by the empire, Luke learned the ways of the force from Obi-Wan Kenobi and helped him save the Princess of Alderann Leia Organa, with the added help of Han Solo, Chewbacca, R2-D2, and C-3PO. While saving the princess from the death star, Obi-Wan sacrifices himself to the villain Darth Vader, so Luke and the rest of the crew can escape successfully. Ben's death inspires, Luke to return to the death star and blow it up using a lazer blast from his spaceship the X-Wing.

Three years after the destruction of the death star, Luke continues to train to become a Jedi. He is told by the force ghost of Obi-Wan, to travel to the planet Dagobah, so he can be taught by Yoda. For several months Luke trains with Yoda in the ways of the force but leaves after sensing his friends are in danger. Luke, lands on the planet Bespin where his friends are but is instead greeted by Darth Vader who has set a trap for him. Luke and Vader have an epic duel until, Lukes hand is cut off by Vader. Vader then tells Luke that he is his father when prievously Obi-Wan told Luke, his father had been killed. Vader also asks, Luke to rule the galaxy with him, as father and son, but without a moment of hesitation Luke falls off a high ledge to his death refusing the dark-side of the force. While falling Luke is caught by his friends except for Han who was placed in carbonite and was for the time replaced with Lando.

One year later, Luke has become a powerful Jedi and user of the force and plots a scheme to get his friend Han back from the crimelord Jabba the Hut. He and the remainder of his friends do so and return to their rebel base. Luke then leaves them to redeem his father because he senses there is still good in him. Luke is successful in redeeming his father as well as defeating the Emporer. All these events culminate into the end of the evil empire and for the first time in a long time balance has been brought to the force.
